Two major features of the supersonic finance ecosystem.

Multi-Chain

We have deployed an open-source blockchain platform created to build and deploy private blockchain applications that function within or between organisations. Project developers are expected to take advantage of our platform to get the best features within one space while interacting with other networks.

Cross Chain

Projects deployed within our framework can take advantage of our cross-chain swap solutions to transact with other chains and projects making the supersonic finance ecosystem. The idea is to build a platform which offers the best Defi features accessible by developers and end users within the same ecosystem.
